What is Triarchs of Olympus?

A Free-for-All Battle for Olympus

Triarchs of Olympus pits three rival empires — Azure, Crimson and Verdant — against one another.

Take command of one champion from a roster of eight, pushing lanes alongside your mob waves, felling enemy towers, contesting labyrinth camps and capture points, and hunting the powerful bosses that ascend from Hade's domain. There's no room for alliances here — every empire fights for itself. The last acropolis left standing wins.

Matches can be filled with humans or AI rivals - There's never a shortage of opponents.

Why the Empires Fight

The War for Olympus

Three empires — Azure, Crimson and Verdant — each lay claim to Olympus, and each has raised its own acropolis and champions to press that claim. There is no truce to break here, only a mountain that will answer to one ruler alone. Whichever empire's acropolis still stands when the others have fallen rules Olympus.

Every champion who takes the field carries their own reasons for fighting beneath an empire's banner — a duellist's endless search for a worthy opponent, a huntress's inherited discipline, an exile's grief, a Titan-touched caster's fire that is slowly killing him. Whatever brought them to the battlefield, when the horns sound, only victory matters.

Azure Empire

Chosen by prophecy to restore order, Azure seeks the throne of Olympus as a sacred duty. Its champions fight to impose peace upon a world they believe has suffered chaos for too long.

Crimson Empire

Crimson recognises no authority except strength. Its champions have come to seize Olympus in open battle and prove, before gods and mortals alike, that victory alone grants the right to rule.

Verdant Empire

Verdant remembers an age before Olympus belonged to kings. Its champions fight to reclaim the mountain for the living earth and awaken the forgotten powers buried beneath its temples.

Built for Three-Way War

Free-for-All

No fixed team split. Azure, Crimson and Verdant each fight for themselves, filled by any mix of human and AI players over a direct peer-to-peer connection.

Lanes & Towers

Watchtowers guard the attack lanes. Destroy them to advance your approach.

Capture Points

Gain favour for your empire by capturing and holding religious sites, spread across the labyrinth.

Hand-Crafted Labyrinth

A maze of grass thickets and neutral camps, each guarded by different creatures.

The Stalking Horror

A wandering Labyrinth hunter patrols the inner battlefield, ambushing isolated heroes.

Timed Bosses

Powerful creatires rise in the central labyrinth. Felling them impresses the Gods, and empowers your whole empire, for a time.

Eight Champions, Five Abilities

Choose from eight original heroes, each with a unique five-ability loadout.

Shop & Ascension

Spend gold at the Agora of the Empire for items, and Skill Points at the Temple of Ascension, whenever you're home.
