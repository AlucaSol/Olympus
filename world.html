Strategic Ground

Key Locations

The Three Acropolises

Each empire's home base and win condition. Heroes are fully healed inside their own base's zone, and a base takes zero damage from enemy heroes — only lane mobs and authorised summons can bring one down.

Capture Point Altars

Three walled sanctums — the Altar of War, Altar of Stone and Altar of Wind — sit off-lane, each defended by three Altar Guardians. Standing uncontested for 8 seconds captures the altar for your empire; progress decays if you abandon the attempt.

The Labyrinth

The walled arena at the exact centre of the map. From the ten-minute mark, a great presence stirs here roughly every ten minutes, spawning one of the two bosses.

The Jungle Maze

A hand-authored web of corridors, chokepoints and long grass connecting the lanes, home to four kinds of neutral camp and the wandering hunter.

Creatures of the Battlefield

Mobs, Camps & Bosses

Standard lane forces, neutral jungle camps, the wandering hunter, and the two timed bosses — with the real buffs and rewards each grants.

Lane Mobs

Waves of these march down each lane every 25 seconds after the first spawn at 0:05, growing stronger as the match goes on.

Hoplite

The standard melee wave mob, marching in numbers behind its shield.

Slinger

Ranged wave support that pelts foes from behind the Hoplites.

Champion

A far stronger mob that reinforces every third wave.

Raider

A fast, aggressive mob that joins the waves from the fourteen-minute mark onward.

Neutral Camps

Four distinct camps live in the jungle, one of each kind per empire's territory. Cyclops Hollow and Sphinx Perch grant a temporary empire buff on top of gold and experience; Creek Nest and Boar Den grant gold and experience alone.

Cave Grub — Creek Nest

Small camp; respawns 3 minutes after being cleared.

Bristleback Boar — Boar Den

Medium camp; respawns 4 minutes after being cleared.

Cyclops — Cyclops Hollow

Strong camp; respawns 5 minutes after being cleared.

Buff: +15% attack, +8 armor for 90s

Sphinx — Sphinx Perch

Specialist camp; respawns 5 minutes after being cleared.

Buff: +12% speed, +10% ability power for 75s

Altar Guardian

Three guard each capture point and must be cleared before it can be captured. Respawn just over 2 minutes after all three fall.

The Wandering Hunter

A single, singular threat that roams a loop through the inner and mid jungle.

The Stalking Horror

A powerful beast that ambushes isolated heroes for bonus damage when it strikes from unseen, but flees to recover once badly wounded. It returns 3 minutes 20 seconds after being slain.

Bosses

From the ten-minute mark, one of two bosses rises in the Labyrinth at the map's centre, alternating between spawns, with a warning thirty seconds before it appears. Whichever empire lands the killing blow gains a powerful, empire-wide buff.

Talandros, the Bronze Colossus

A slow, devastating boss that periodically slams the ground around itself for heavy area damage.

Chthonia, Maw of the Deep

A long-ranged boss that periodically unleashes a devastating breath attack down a corridor.

Boss buff: for 90 seconds, the killer's empire deals +25% damage to enemies with their lane mobs, and those mobs take 50% less damage from enemy towers.
